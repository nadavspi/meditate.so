  • Meditation comprises a variety of techniques that target
    Meditation comprises a variety of techniques that target the functioning of the mind. It can lead to profound perceptual and cognitive transformations that have traditonally been called spiritual enlightenment or awakening.

    Turtle: It might be more accurate to say *enlightenments* or *awaken-ing*, since the results of meditation are neither uniform nor steady state.

  • Meditation can make you feel good, but it can also
    Meditation can make you feel good, but it can also be boring, stressful, and frustrating at times.

    Turtle: It’s not a linear development of feeling better and better.

  • Not about stopping thoughts
    Meditation is not about stopping your thoughts. Success is not determined by how few thoughts you have.

    Turtle: You might be shocked by how many thoughts you notice as soon as you sit down to meditate. That’s okay!
